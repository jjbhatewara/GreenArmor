Our Technology

Chemical-free cellular processing, at industrial scale

Proprietary enzyme treatment transforms agricultural banana leaf waste into high-performance packaging composites — durable, water-resistant, and fully compostable.

1

Sourcing — Agri-waste Collection

Banana pruning waste collected from farming cooperatives across Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, and Maharashtra. Zero-cost feedstock that would otherwise be field-burned.

2

Processing — Cellular Enhancement

Proprietary enzyme treatment extends natural shelf life from 3 days to 12 months. AI-driven sorting ensures uniform thickness for industrial packaging lines. 40% faster than traditional curing methods.

3

Manufacturing — B2B Production

Decentralised micro-processing hubs near plantation zones reduce logistics cost and transit time. Output meets FSSAI food contact standards and EPR exemption criteria.

4

Distribution — Direct to Clients

Multi-year offtake agreements with FMCG, food delivery platforms, and agribusiness exporters. Just-in-time delivery with 30-day inventory buffer for supply security.
